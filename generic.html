Hoot Hoot Auto Security is the proud primary auto security provider in the nation!

How we got started

Hoot Hoot was established in 2021 by our original founders: Rolland Loy, Steve Stamp, and Kenneth Clark. "We started this company because we see poor auto security as a huge threat to many people. I believe that it is important for the general public to feel confident that their main methods of transportation are secured and protected"- Steve. We at Hoot Hoot all feel the same way. Auto security is finally being adressed with a trustworthy and capable solution. Join the Hoot Hoot Auto family today and start feeling safe about your automobile.
